You are given a staircase with n number of steps.
You can climb the steps either 1, or 2 steps at a time.
In how many unique ways can you climb to the top of the stair case

Sample Input:
n = 2
Output:
2
There are 2 ways to climb the staircase
(1, 1) and (2)

Sample Input:
n = 4
Output:
5
(1, 1, 1, 1), (2, 2), (2, 1, 1), (1, 2, 1), (1, 1, 2)
