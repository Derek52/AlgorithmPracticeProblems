You are given an array of integers, and are tasked with figuring out how many jumps it will take to get to the end.
Starting at index 0, you may jump X times, where X equals the number at the current index.


Example Input:
[3, 2, 5, 1, 1, 9, 3, 4]
Answer:
2 jumps
3 -> 5 -> 4


Example Input:
[3, 2, 1, 2, 1, 2, 3, 4, 1, 2, 1]
Answer:
4 jumps
3 -> 2 -> 2 -> 4 -> 1
